# BMC Complementary Medicine and Therapies — 1/2025 Evidence Snapshot

**Context:**  
This document summarizes key findings reported in **BMC Complementary Medicine and Therapies 1/2025** as described in the linked issue overview. It is designed as a **journal evidence snapshot** to layer on top of existing monographs and clinical guides without repeating baseline pharmacology or traditional-use information.

All bullet points below reflect the wording and scope of the BMC 1/2025 summary and **should not be interpreted as treatment recommendations**. For implementation or safety questions, professional medical guidance is required.

---

## Cardiovascular & Metabolic Health

- **Pine bark extract**: May improve cardiometabolic risk factors in hypertensive patients.
- **Calcium, magnesium, and vitamin D supplementation**: Shows promise for managing hypertension.
- **Wogonoside**: A component of traditional Chinese medicine with potential in treating atherosclerosis.
- **Safflower yellow injection**: Activates protective mechanisms against myocardial injury.
- **Shenmai injection**: Improves cardiac function and modulates gut microbiome in hypertensive heart failure.
- **Ellagic acid (250–500 mg/day)**: Improves glycemic status, liver enzymes, and oxidative stress in NAFLD.
- **Phyllanthus niruri + Silybum marianum (milk thistle)**: Beneficial activity against NASH.
- **Garlic & stevia plus aerobic exercise**: Improves insulin resistance and appetite hormones.
- **Marjoram (_Origanum majorana_)**: Therapeutic for PCOS via metabolic improvements.

---

## Mental Health & Neurological Conditions

- **Melissa officinalis (lemon balm) essential oil**: Reduces anxiety in hemodialysis patients.
- **Lemongrass (_Cymbopogon citratus_) aromatherapy**: Lowers dental procedure anxiety.
- **Music therapy**: Reduces perioperative anxiety and stabilizes physiological responses.
- **Mindfulness-based cognitive therapy (MBCT)**: Improves depression outcomes in older adults.
- **Tangeretin (citrus flavonoid)**: Neuroprotective against memory impairment, reduces inflammation.
- **Rosemary (_Rosmarinus officinalis_)**: Protects against cell apoptosis in Alzheimer's models.
- **Cedrol**: Provides neuroprotection during cerebral ischemia/reperfusion injury.
- **Liuzijue Qigong**: Improves respiratory function in Parkinson’s disease.

---

## Cancer Support

- **Quercetin**: Triggers apoptosis in oral cancer cells.
- **Ursolic acid**: Induces apoptosis in ovarian cancer cells.
- **Curcumin & shogaol**: Show activity against leukemic stem cells.
- **Pistachio hull extract + carfilzomib**: Affects breast cancer resistance pathways.
- **Mushroom β-glucan/paclitaxel**: Demonstrates synergistic anticancer potential.
- **Pomegranate compounds**: Inhibit lung cancer cell propagation.
- **_Rosa canina_ (rosehip) + gamma-irradiation**: Show antitumor effect in lung cancer.
- **Sweetpotato extracts**: Exhibit antiproliferative effects in lung cancer.
- **_Senna didymobotrya_ leaf alkaloids**: Anti-proliferative for breast cancer.
- **_Sarcandra glabra_ compounds**: Target multiple pathways in pancreatic cancer.

---

## Pain & Physical Rehabilitation

- **Traditional Thai massage**: Comparable efficacy to ultrasound therapy for plantar heel pain.
- **Mechanical traction**: Shows promise for cervical radiculopathy relief.
- **Acupuncture**: Used widely among chronic disease patients in primary care.
- **Transcutaneous acupoint electrical stimulation**: Reduces postoperative nausea/vomiting.
- **Tai chi**: Helps immunosenescence in prefrail older adults.
- **Yoga**: Improves cardiovascular, respiratory, and mental health outcomes.
- **Qigong**: Supports physical and mental health in diverse populations.
- **Daoyin training**: Improves posture and pulmonary function.

---

## Respiratory & Post-COVID

- **Teleyoga**: Feasible for post-COVID patients.
- **Balneotherapy (spa therapy)**: Provides benefits for post-COVID syndrome.
- **_Zataria multiflora_**: Improves pulmonary symptoms and function.
- **Chungsangboha-tang + leukotriene antagonists**: Investigated for asthma treatment.

---

## Antimicrobial Applications

- **Clove extract**: Effective against _Pseudomonas aeruginosa_.
- **Medical-grade honey (Iranian)**: Demonstrates antibacterial and anti-biofilm efficacy for multidrug-resistant _Pseudomonas_.
- **_Acacia catechu_ extracts**: Show urobactericidal and antibiotic-modulating effects.
- **Hibiscus (_H. sabdariffa_) calyces**: Display antibacterial activity.
- **Indonesian plants**: Show activity against _Giardia intestinalis_.
- **_Enantia chlorantha_**: Exhibits antimalarial potential.
- **_Andrographis paniculata_ flavonoids**: Modulate hepatitis B virus replication.

---

## Children’s Health

- **Play therapy and storytelling**: Reduce anxiety in hospitalized children.
- **Herbal supplements**: Often preferred by parents for children with functional GI disorders.

---

## Women’s Health

- **Traditional/complementary medicine**: Used for menopause symptom management.
- **Herbal remedies**: Reported as efficacious for gynecological ailments.
- **Anzi Tiaochong Fang**: Mechanistic evidence for preventing recurrent pregnancy loss (APS-linked).

---

## Wound Healing & Skin

- **Pulsed electromagnetic fields (PEMF)**: Modulate metabolism during wound healing.
- **Botanical extracts**: Specific botanicals improve wound healing and reduce infection risk.
- **Honey phenolic acids**: Show potential for skin permeation in topical formulations.
- **Shilajit**: Promotes periodontal ligament wound healing.

---

## Oxidative Stress & Inflammation

- **Golden berry (_Physalis peruviana_) calyces**: Reduce oxidative stress and inflammation.
- **_Syzygium guineense_ bark extracts**: Exhibit antioxidant and antibacterial effects.
- **Blackcurrant leaf extract**: Downregulates inflammation and regulates redox balance.
- **Sesamol**: Protects the blood–brain barrier from cigarette smoke damage.

---

## Gut Health

- **Arhatic yoga meditation**: Produces rapid positive shifts in the gut microbiome.
- **Kratom extract**: Modulates gut microbiota.

---

## Chronic Fatigue

- **Qigong Tuina therapy**: Under study for chronic fatigue syndrome.
