## FAQs (Quick Answers)

**"How long until herbs work?"**
- Acute symptoms (nausea, gas): 15-30 minutes
- Sleep/anxiety: 1-4 weeks
- Immune support: 2-4 weeks
- Chronic conditions: 4-8 weeks

**"Can I take herbs with my medications?"**
- Most are safe, but ALWAYS check [Herb Safety Basics](./Herb-Safety-Basics.md)
- Tell your doctor and pharmacist
- Some herbs have significant interactions (St. John's Wort!)

**"Where do I buy herbs?"**
- See [How to Buy Quality Herbs](./How-To-Buy-Quality-Herbs.md)
- Recommended: Mountain Rose Herbs, Starwest Botanicals, Frontier Co-op
- Avoid: Gas stations, sketchy Amazon sellers

**"How much do herbs cost?"**
- Starter kit (10 herbs): $40-60
- Monthly maintenance: $15-30
- Much cheaper than most supplements or medications

**"Are herbs safe for kids/pregnancy?"**
- Some are, some aren't
- Read [Herb Safety Basics](./Herb-Safety-Basics.md) special populations section
- When in doubt, consult healthcare provider

**"Do I need to see an herbalist?"**
- Not for common concerns using this guide
- Helpful for complex health issues
- Necessary for serious conditions
