# Q24: Is there a herb that can help emotional stress without making me sleepy?

**Quick Answer:** Yes! Adaptogens are perfect for this - they help with stress WITHOUT causing drowsiness. Rhodiola (*Rhodiola rosea*) is energizing and reduces stress (Grade A-B evidence). Holy basil (*Ocimum sanctum*) provides stress relief with mild energizing effects (Grade B evidence). Ashwagandha (*Withania somnifera*) reduces stress without sedation (Grade B evidence). These herbs are ideal for daytime stress management when you need to stay alert.

### 1. Rhodiola (*Rhodiola rosea*) - Grade A-B Evidence

**Best For:** Stress relief with energy boost, mental fatigue from stress, daytime stress management

- **Timing:** Morning (energizing, may affect sleep if taken late)
- **Duration:** 4-8 weeks for full effects
- **Cycling:** Some practitioners recommend 8-12 weeks on, 2-4 weeks off

**Timeline:** Stress reduction noticeable within 2-4 weeks
