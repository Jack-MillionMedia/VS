# Q26: What Herbal Remedy Might Help Skin Healing After a Minor Burn or Cut?

**Quick Answer:** Aloe vera (*Aloe barbadensis*) is the most evidence-supported herb for minor burns (Grade A-B evidence), providing immediate cooling and promoting healing. Lavender essential oil (Grade B evidence) is excellent for burns - analgesic and antiseptic. Calendula (*Calendula officinalis*) promotes wound healing for cuts (Grade B evidence). Comfrey (*Symphytum officinale*) accelerates healing but must be PA-free and topical only. The key is proper application and knowing when to seek medical care.

---

## Detailed Evidence-Based Answer

### Understanding Wound Types

**Minor Burns:**
- First-degree (redness, pain)
- Superficial second-degree (blisters, pain)
- Best herbs: Aloe vera, lavender (cooling, healing)

**Minor Cuts/Scrapes:**
- Superficial wounds
- Clean cuts
- Best herbs: Calendula, comfrey (healing, tissue repair)

**CRITICAL:** Serious burns (deep, large area, third-degree) or deep cuts require immediate medical care.

## First Aid Protocols

### Minor Burn Protocol

**Immediate (First 10-15 minutes):**
1. **Cool the burn:** Cool (not ice cold) running water for 10-15 minutes
2. **Do NOT use ice** - Can cause further tissue damage
3. **Remove clothing/jewelry** - If not stuck to burn

**After Cooling:**
1. **Aloe vera gel:** Apply liberally to burn area
2. **OR Lavender oil:** Diluted (2-3 drops in carrier oil), apply gently
3. **Cover if needed:** Loose, non-stick bandage
4. **Reapply:** 2-3 times daily until healed

**Timeline:** Pain relief immediate; healing within 3-7 days

**When to Seek Medical Care:**
- üî¥ **Large burns** (>3 inches diameter)
- üî¥ **Deep burns** (third-degree)
- üî¥ **Burns on face, hands, feet, genitals**
- üî¥ **Signs of infection** (increasing redness, pus, fever)

---

### Minor Cut/Scrape Protocol

**Immediate:**
1. **Clean wound:** Rinse with clean water
2. **Stop bleeding:** Apply gentle pressure
3. **Remove debris:** Gently remove visible dirt/debris

**After Cleaning:**
1. **Calendula cream:** Apply to clean wound
2. **OR Comfrey cream (PA-free):** Apply for accelerated healing
3. **Cover:** Clean bandage if needed
4. **Reapply:** 2-3 times daily until healed

**Timeline:** Healing within 5-7 days for minor cuts

**When to Seek Medical Care:**
- üî¥ **Deep cuts** (may need stitches)
- üî¥ **Heavy bleeding** (doesn't stop with pressure)
- üî¥ **Signs of infection** (redness, swelling, pus, fever)
- üî¥ **Tetanus concerns** (dirty wound, >5 years since last shot)
