*   **How to Use:** One of the few essential oils that can be applied "neat" (undiluted) to small burns, but dilution is always safer.

*   **How to Use:** **EXTREME CAUTION.** Dilute to 1% (1 drop in a tablespoon of oil). Never apply undiluted to skin.
