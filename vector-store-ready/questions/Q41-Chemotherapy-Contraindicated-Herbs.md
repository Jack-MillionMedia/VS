# Q41: What Herbs are Contraindicated During Chemotherapy?

**Quick Answer:** St. John's Wort is ABSOLUTELY CONTRAINDICATED during chemotherapy (reduces drug levels, treatment failure risk). Immune-stimulating herbs (echinacea, astragalus) should generally be avoided during active chemotherapy. High-dose antioxidants are controversial - some may interfere with chemotherapy effectiveness. However, ginger is SAFE and beneficial for chemotherapy-induced nausea. Milk thistle is SAFE and may be hepatoprotective. The key is coordinating ALL herbal use with your oncology team - never use herbs during chemotherapy without medical approval.
